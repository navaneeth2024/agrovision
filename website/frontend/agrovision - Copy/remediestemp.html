Remedies for Plant Leaf

Remedy 1: Natural Pesticides

Use natural pesticides such as neem oil or garlic spray to control pests and diseases without harming the environment.

Remedy 2: Crop Rotation

Rotate crops to disrupt the life cycle of pests and diseases, reducing their prevalence in the soil.

Remedy 3: Proper Watering

Avoid overwatering as it can lead to fungal diseases. Water plants at the base in the morning to allow leaves to dry out during the day.
